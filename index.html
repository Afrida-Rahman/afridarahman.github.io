About

I am Afrida Rahman, completed my graduation in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), Bangladesh. I have gathered around 1 year 6 months working experience as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer in a renowned US healthcare company that provides musculoskeletal health management with effective AI solutions. Here, I have been involved with the Learned Therapist team and developed an “AI Coach” (real-time musculoskeletal AI-controlled mobile application) for those patients who have musculoskeletal health issues. Aside from that, I have been working as a researcher in the “Machine Learning Research Lab” for 3 years and conducted my research on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Sequence Data Analysis, Data Preprocessing with Data Visualisation, Feature Engineering etc. I have 5 publications(4 Journal articles and 1 Conference paper) on Bioinformatics and Machine Learning research and deployed research works on user-friendly web-server. Now, I am working on Medical Imaging, Gesture Detection, RNA modification and Cancer Detection projects. Furthermore, I have worked on some DevOps scenarios,such as Docker, to containerize different applications. Completed some web development projects using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, Python, Docker, Django, kotlin.
